About Static Quo

Static Quo is a static site generator that allows you to quickly and easily create simple, fast, and beautiful websites. With Static Quo, you don't need to worry about complicated backend systems or databases - simply create your content in Markdown or HTML, upload it to the console, and let Static Quo do the rest. Whether you're creating a personal blog, a portfolio site, or a small business website, Static Quo has everything you need to get started.
